Bingo Rules

1. Winning cards must have the last number called in pattern
2. All bingo players must be 18 Years of age, ID may be required
3. All players must have a session buy-in and receipt. No splitting of packages
4. It is the PLAYER'S responsibility to YELL bingo loud enough for Caller to Hear. If the caller does not acknowledge you, YELL again
5. No bingos will be honored after the caller doses the game
6. Flash games will be played with bingo/strip games
7. Ink daubers must be used on all games
8. All prizes will be split evenly between winners
9. Broadway Bingo is not responsible for malfunction of equipment
10. Number is "dead" when the caller begins the letter or number of next ball
11. All decisions made by the manager are final.
